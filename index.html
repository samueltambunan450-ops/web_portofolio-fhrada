About me

A learning journey that I keep turning into real work.

I enjoy turning my learning process into projects that can be clearly presented, from static websites and Laravel development to mobile app collaboration.

I am an aspiring programmer who keeps growing through college projects and independent exploration. In my early semester, I was trusted to lead a team in building a Medan culinary website using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and PHP.

That journey continued in the following semester when the project was developed further into a Laravel-based Medan culinary recommendation website. From there, I learned how to structure application flows in a cleaner and more ready-to-use way.

I was also involved in the WorkRadar mobile project, especially on the weather and location features using the Open-Meteo API and Geolocator. Besides my main projects, I have also built a simple calculator and a digital wedding invitation to strengthen my application development fundamentals.
